Process

1. Discover
At Wash we work with organisations and people who understand their brand is important. Without understanding your brand you can’t have effective communications.
So when we look at your business we adopt what your organisation is, why you exist and importantly what you want to achieve. We do this in conjunction with looking at your market and audience. This allows us to command a great integrated presence that can help achieve your business goals, be focused on the people you wish to reach.

2. Plan
Once an objective is agreed at ‘discovery’, a key part of making your project successful is defining how we intend to execute. We interpret the ‘discovery’ outcomes to form goals for the project, outlining the proposed structure to aid in achieving this.

3. Create
With a solid plan in place, this allows the creative team to create an inspiring and exciting concept with the added benefit that all the thought as to what elements are required having been completed.
Similarly, the technical solution is configured to match the specification already outlined in the planning stage. This process makes for an efficient project getting maximum impact for the budget set.

4. Deliver
Only after evaluation and testing will the project be ready for launch.
Our job does not end there though and we care about all our projects. Our monitoring service allows us to ensure your project is performing against its objectives and we pro-actively communicate on its status and possible future developments.
